About Me

I am a Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at IIT Roorkee. My research interests include compiler optimizations, program analysis, software security and machine learning for systems. Prior to this, I worked in Qualcoom and AMD in the similar domain.

Research Interests

  • Software Testing and Verification
  • Compilers and Program Analysis
  • Machine Learning for Systems
  • Software security and Reliability
